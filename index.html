About Me

Hello! I'm Vishwajeet, a Ph.D. student and Early Stage Researcher with the Information Systems Group at Tallinn University of Technology, Estonia. And, as part of ongoing Ph.D. research, I am developing a Collective Intelligence platform for the web, that would allow citizens to critique and curate web content while aiming to democratise the Web.

I have a Masters Degree in Computer Science with specialization in Knowledge Engineering from SRM University, Chennai. Before starting my Ph.D., I worked as an Assistant Professor at A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University for 3 years, and taught subjects including Automata, Data Structure, Graph Theory, Machine Learning and Web Technologies. During the same time, I was also a Research Fellow at IIT Delhi(for a short period), where I worked on Medical Image Processing and Machine Learning.
